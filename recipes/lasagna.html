  • 500g Ground beef
  • 1 Onions
  • 2 Garlic cloves
  • 1 Can crushed tomatos
  • food creeam
  • 2 msk tomatpure
  • salt
  • peppar
  • white pepper
  • lasnaga pasta
  • 7 dl milk
  • 3 dl chees
  • 1/2 dl butter
  • 2 msk flouer

    beef sås

  1. start by chop the onion and start the oven on 200-225c
  2. put the onions on low heat in the pan and when they are done put in the grounded beef
  3. Put in spices after your own taste
  4. Lower heat on the stow and put in the smased tomatos, creeam,tomatepurer and garlic.

    5. chees sås

  5. you need a castrull to melt the butter then put in the flouer while you stair it
  6. put in the milk and then the chees then some slat and pepper
  7. stair til the chees have melted and the sås have got a thick consistens

    8. lasanga

  8. butter up a form then put beef sås, chees sås and lasanga pasta in layers and put in the owen for 35-40 min
  9. when done put on tabel and get amazed by the creemynes
