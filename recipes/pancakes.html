Pancakes

This is my special way to do pankakes follow my tips and tricks to get the best panckaes of them all

this is what you need to make the pancakes

  • 5 dl Flouer
  • 5 tsk sugar
  • 2 tsk salt
  • 5 egg
  • 9 dl milk
  • butter for the pan

Follow thees steps to make the best pancakaes batter!

  1. Start to put all the dry ingredients in a bucket and mix
  2. Take a new bucket and put 5 dl milk in it and then mix slowly till it is a smooth batter
  3. Put in the rest of the milk and mix in it and then put in the 5 eggs and mix them in
  4. pre heat the pan and when hot put in the butter and the batter
  5. then repeat this till all the batter is done and serve it with jam, vanilla icecream or/and wipecreem
