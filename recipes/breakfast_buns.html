breakfast buns

This is a fast and tasty breakfast bread that will make you go up erlier evry day to make your own bread for your breakfast

  • 5 dl flouer
  • 0.25 msk salt
  • 1 msk baking Soda
  • 2 msk light syrup
  • 2.5 dl water

start by put the owen on 200c

  1. Put all the dry ingredice in a bucket and mix it
  2. Then use the backside of msk and make two hole whare you can put the syrup
  3. Put in the water and then mix it all together
  4. make 10 round balls that you make more flat and put them on the plate
  5. put the plate in the middle of the owen for 12-15min
  6. enjoy whit butter and chees or your favorit toppings
